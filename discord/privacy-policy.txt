PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 12/05/24

Thank you for using FortiBot! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with FortiBot on Discord. By using FortiBot, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.


---

1. Data We Collect

FortiBot collects only the minimum data necessary to function effectively:

a. Discord Data

User ID: Used to identify who is interacting with the bot.

Server ID: Used to manage bot functionalities specific to each server.

Message Content: Commands sent to the bot for processing and responding.


b. Fortnite Data

Public Fortnite Profile Information: Retrieved from the Fortnite API based on user requests (e.g., player stats, leaderboard positions).


We do not collect sensitive personal data such as email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords.


---

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve FortiBot’s services:

Responding to user commands and providing requested information.

Customizing features and functionalities based on server settings.

Monitoring and ensuring the bot’s proper operation and performance.


We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.


---

3. Data Storage and Retention

Discord data (such as User ID and Server ID) is stored temporarily to process commands and ensure proper functionality.

No long-term data storage is performed unless required for specific bot features (e.g., leaderboard rankings).

Data is deleted when it is no longer necessary for the bot’s functionality or if the bot is removed from the server.



---

4. Data Sharing

We may share data in the following cases:

With Discord: To interact with their services as part of normal bot functionality.

With Fortnite API: To retrieve requested Fortnite data.

Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others.


We do not share data with third parties for any commercial purpose.


---

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Request access to the data collected by FortiBot.

Request deletion of your data, except where it is necessary for FortiBot’s core functionalities.

Stop using the bot at any time to prevent further data collection.


To make a request, please contact us via our support channels.


---

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.


---

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated via the bot’s support server or a Discord update. Continued use of FortiBot after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
